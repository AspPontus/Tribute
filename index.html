Who is Jack?

Jack Black, or Thomas Jacob Black, as his name is, is an american actor, and musician. Jack was born in August 1969 to his mother Judith Love Cohen. Who worked on the system that helped save the Apollo 13 austronauts. Jack is well known for his roles in films like School of rock, Gulliver's Travels and Kung fu panda! But Black is also known for his musical act, Tenacious D. Black is the lead vocalist of the comedy rock duo. Tenacious D was formed along with his long time friend Kyle Gass

Black has through his career gained grammy awards as well as been nominated in the golden globe awards. Black recently got a star in the Hollywood walk of fame, and also started a youtube channel by the name of Jablinski Games!
